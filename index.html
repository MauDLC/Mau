English route

From basic foundations to B2 with a clear map

Basic Survival foundations

Pronouns, be, present simple, questions and essential vocabulary.

A1 Functional base

Routines, descriptions, prepositions, there is/are and short listening.

A2 Pre-intermediate

Past, future, comparatives, quantifiers and reading for detail.

B1 Intermediate

Perfect tenses, modals, conditionals, connectors and inference.

B2 Upper-intermediate

Passive voice, reported speech, collocations and academic reading.

  • Update weak-topic list.

B1 deep work

Use the longer block for B1 structures and content filling.

  • Present perfect, modals and first/second conditional.

Sun, Jun 14 · flexible

B2 foundations

B2 reading + vocabulary

Work on tone, inference, collocations and phrasal verbs.

  • One longer reading passage.
  • Collocations and word formation list.
  • Add difficult questions to review bank.

Full mock + review

Targeted repair

Final review

Keep it calm: strategy, error log and light practice only.

Test day

No cramming. Warm up lightly and protect your focus.
